KNOWLEDGE EXAMS

Administered by CompassLZ.com

We currently offer two exams.  The LZ Officer's Knowledge Exam certifies first responders for safe and efficient LZ operations.  While our Master LZ Officer's Knowledge Exam certifies HAA professionals and industry consultants.

PRICING

Safety-minded teams, that earn and maintain annual Compass™ LZ Certifications, keep fellow first responders and their communities safer.

Leveraging CompassLZ.com's online certification process benefits everyone that supports safe and efficent LZ operations, whether on the ground, from the air, or in the classroom.

  • First Responders
  • HAA Team Members
  • HAA Consultants

SAFER LANDING ZONES

Fire, police, and EMS departments that certify LZ Officer coverage for each shift insist upon the highest safety standards for their communities and forces.

ONLINE EXAM

Whether from the classroom or a mobile device, Compass™ individually paced online certification exams convieniently enable annual department certification.

ANNUAL CERTIFICATES

Showcase your achievement on CompassLZ.com's LZ Officer's Leader Board worldwide and see when to renew a soon to expire certification.

MASS CERTIFICATION

Certifiying the "boots on the ground" can now happen efficiently online thru our Membership Services where we also provide data about soon to expire certifications.

SAFE EMPLOYEES

Be certain that your managers, pilots, and specialist know essential safety protocols and have the depth of knowledge to support any LZ operations scenario.

IMPROVE COSTS

Using a CompassLZ.com for certification reduces personnel travel and equipment utilization for training significantly saving expenses for cost conscious companies.

REACH GLOBALLY

Consultants listed on our leader board, that have earned a Master LZ Officer Certificate, recieve more business from our customers maintaining and expanding HAA programs.

MORE OPPORTUNITY

We make available to our customers, at the Master LZ Officer Certificate level, rich data about under-scoring companies where consultation will make the most impact. 

PROVEN EXPERTISE

Those who earn a Master LZ Officer Certificate prove that they have the experience and skills to educate thier client's personnel about safe LZ operations.
