How does it work?

Three easy steps that result in safer LZ operations for all

Purchase Membership Package

CompassLZ.com helps ensure safer LZ operations through membership packaging.  When you purchasae Compass™ Membership Packages, you receive discount pricing, helping you support a worthy cause. To benefit the industry and communities even further, we donate a portion to charities that promote HAA safety.

Recieve Exam Code

Some communities do not have adequate funding to train or certify their first responders for safe LZ operations–let us help! Our Compass™ Membership Packages allow companies to fund Compass™ Knowledge Exams for others. Once purchased, we work with you to create unique access codes for your students' exams.

Students Take Exams

Your students then login using their unique access codes, on a computer or mobile device, to take their online Compass™ Knowledge Exams. With no additional costs, this easy process requires no credit card information from your students. CompassLZ.com provides data about your student's scores supporting wise spending of training funds where it matters most.
