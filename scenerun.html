CompassLZ.com Launches

Answering the Calling for Improved Helicopter Air Ambulance (HAA) Landing Zone Safety 

Now certifying LZ Officers! 
The CompassLZ.com team came together around the vision to help eradicate preventable HAA disasters that still plague our industry and people. We do this by taking aim at a U.S. Federal Aviation Administration calling to address an increase in fatal HAA accidents by implementing improved safety cultures, as detailed in AC No. 135 14-B. As a result, CompassLZ.com devised a better way to train and verifiably certify LZ Officers annually. READ MORE.

The LZ Officer's Handbook

Available NOW on Amazon for Kindle and in Paperback!

The first complete LZ training resource! 
The Landing Zone Officer’s Handbook, A Comprehensive Selection of Helicopter Air Ambulance Industry Standards, Recommendations, and Best Practices,—initially compiled as a study guide for the LZ Officer Knowledge Exams administered by CompassLZ.com for the annual certification of LZ Officers and Master LZ Officers—evolved from a U.S. Federal Aviation Administration call-to-action, concerning program development for robust landing zone training. This handbook provides first responders, such as EMS, fire, and law enforcement agencies, supporting air medical transport as part of their mission profile, a comprehensive overview of safe landing zone (LZ) operations practices. It also serves as a reference guide for helicopter air ambulance (HAA) companies and industry consultants for training employees and first responders. BUY NOW or READ MORE.
