The LZ Officer Knowledge Exam

is a 60 question exam administered within an 80-minute time limit.

Each Exam is Unique

Containing 60 random questions, with randomized answers, drawn from a question bank of 1000+ questions. Each of the multiple choice and true/false questions were crafted to challenge the student's general knowledge of LZ operations core components, as broke out in the infographic, to the right, representing the percent of exam questions selected per each category. Each segment builds upon the other to validate a well-rounded LZ situational awareness expected of Certified LZ Officers.

The recommended reading, The Landing Zone Officer's Handbook, provides a complete study guide companion to prepare for the LZ Officer Knowledge Exam.

The Master LZ Officer Knowledge Exam

is a 70 question exam administered within an 90-minute time limit.

Each Exam is Tough

Containing 70 random questions, chosen similarly to the more accessible LZ Officer Knowledge Exam. Each of the multiple choice and true/false questions was crafted to challenge the student's advanced knowledge of LZ operations core components, as broke out in the infographic, to the right, representing the percent of exam questions selected per each category. Each segment builds upon the other to validate an expert-level LZ situational awareness, and training capability, expected of Certified Master LZ Officers.

The recommended reading, The Landing Zone Officer's Handbook, not only provides a complete study guide companion to prepare for the Master LZ Officer Knowledge Exam, but is acclaimed as a resource for trainer's and consultant's LZ operations safety course development and training.
