Safer HAA Operations

How CompassLZ.com was envisioned

The CompassLZ.com team came together around the vision to help eradicate preventable HAA disasters that still plague our industry and people. We do this by aiming to answer a U.S. Federal Aviation Administration calling to address an increase in fatal HAA accidents by implementing improved safety cultures, as detailed in AC No. 135 14-B. As a result, CompassLZ.com devised a better way to train and verifiably certify LZ Officers annually.

With industry roots spanning two decades and advisory from industry experts, the CompassLZ.com team cares deeply about the well-being of our first responders. We have applied behavioral-science techniques, learned at Amazon, for the creation of our distributed adult learning methods. CompassLZ.com's unrivaled experiences and skill positions us well to better help you keep your team safe!

Compass™ Knowledge Exams use the randomization of over one-thousand questions to create a unique self-paced online exam experience for each student. Earning an annual LZ Officer’s Certificate validates the readiness of emergency response forces to successfully engage any LZ Operation that they may encounter. Companies can now train and certify more personnel with limited resources in a modern, cost-effective way.
